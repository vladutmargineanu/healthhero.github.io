Interviews

Also, depending on the evolution of the discussion, we asked additional questions to get more clarifications when we felt the need for more details from the interviewees.
The questions were the following:

Interview of customer 1

Hi and thanks for accepting to talk to us!
Firstly, tell us something about yourself.

Hey! I'm 23 years old and I am working as a Junior Java Developer.
Also, at the same time, I do a master degree in graphics, multimedia and virtual reality.
For what services do you usually make an appointment at a beauty and wellness salon?
Only for haircut.
And you always go to the same barber shop?
At one point I was going, but now not anymore.
Why not anymore?
Because I couldn't find a reliable hairdresser.
I knew my former hairdresser since I was in the high school, but now he is 200km away, considering that I now live in Bucharest.
Since I moved here, I've been trying to find someone with his abilities.
What made your former hairdresser stand out from the others?
He knew how to trim my hair well and he had attention to details.
What didn't you like about the hairdressers you've been to since you moved here?
They didn't cut my hair as I wanted.
They didn't know how to trim using the scissors, but only with the trimmer.
What method do you use to make an appointment and why?
If the hairdresser I want to go to was recommended by a friend, I usually call him directly 3-4 days in advance.
Otherwise, I go personally to the salon to make an appointment.
I want to see how the salon and barber look like to make sure it's reliable.
Do you like to use this methods?
I hate to go personally to the salons.
It consumes me a lot of time and sometimes you have to wait long enough for the hairdresser to finish trimming the client so he can schedule you.
Once I had to wait 30 minutes.
Calling the hairdresser is ok, but I would like to be able to make an appointment online.
What other difficulties are you facing during your interaction with the beauty and wellness salons?
The hairdresser made a mistake and cut a piece of skin off my back.
Maybe he wasn't paying attention for a moment or he was a beginner, I don't know. It happens.
How do you prefer to pay for a service at a beauty salon?
I use to pay cash. I don't mind doing that.
Thank you for your time! Your answers will help us!

Interview of customer 2

Hi and thanks for accepting to talk to us! Firstly, tell us something about yourself.
Hi! I am 23 years old and I’m working as a software developer in a small company.
Also, I like to go to dance classes and nutrition courses.
Tell me something about the services you usually make an appointment at a beauty and wellness salon.
I have my hair cut only 2 or 3 times a year because I want to let it grow longer, but I go more often to manicure and eyebrow shaping (at 3-4 weeks) and currently I started to go to permanent hair removal procedure.
Do you always go to the same beauty and wellness salon?
Currently, yes. I go to the same 3 persons all the time: hairstylist, manicurist and beautician. But this is only because I like that they are professional.
I am used to being a loyal customer if I am offered quality services. Otherwise, I'll start searching for another salon.
And how did you find the current salons?
Recommendations from friends.
What method do you use to make an appointment and why?
I usually send a message a few days in advance and occasionally I make a phone call to the reception of the salon. I tend to send messages more often because the employees at the reception are not very friendly.
What other difficulties are you facing during your interaction with the beauty and wellness salons?
Once I went to a salon and I was told that the stylist I had appointment to can’t come and that someone else will cut my hair. I didn't like that. I would have liked to be announced before so that I can decide whether or not I agree with this change.

I also had problems with the products used by salons. I am vegan and I am against animal tested products, so I want to be sure that when I go to the salon the employees use only vegan products for me. I have asked several times if the used products are vegan and I was told that they are, but later I found out that they were not.
How do you prefer to pay for a service that you have benefited from after a visit to a beauty and wellness salon?
I use to pay cash, but I would like to pay using the credit card. I don’t usually have cash with me, so when I go to the salon I have to remember to get money from the ATM.
You can't pay using the credit card? Or why do you always have to have cash with you when going to the salons?
No, I can only pay cash. The salons I go to have no payment system using the card. You wouldn't say we're in 2019, right?
Yes, you are right about that. Thank you for your time! Your answers will help us!

Interview of beauty and wellness company representant 1

Hi and thanks for accepting to talk to us! Firstly, tell us something about yourself.
Hi! I am 30 years old and I have a business with beauty services.
Also, my hobbies are tattoos.
Tell us about your business.
When I was younger, I worked in a beauty salon as a hair stylist, but I didn't like the atmosphere, so I decided to resign and to open my own business. Now, I am the only employee and I am also my boss! It's the best!
How do you keep track of customers' appointments?
I have an agenda I take notes on.
And this is efficient? Or do you have problems in the management of the appointments?
It’s ok. I can’t say that it’s the best method, but it works ok in my case.
The only problem is that my handwriting is far from being calligraphic and sometimes I don't understand my own writing.
What are the difficulties you face in managing your business and interacting with clients?
What bothers me the most are the clients who make an appointment and don’t announce that they are not coming anymore. That means disrespect to me.
In addition, clients always call me to make an appointment. This can be a little annoying since I'm the only employee. I find it difficult to schedule the appointments when I have a client in my salon: it is unprofessional to answer the phone, so I choose to decline the call, but this is also unprofessional to the caller.
How many clients did not respect their appointments?
I estimate somewhere at 10%. But these 10%, even though it may not seem like much to you, can be translated into wasted time for me, which leads to lost money because I can't get a new customer so fast for that time interval.
Thank you for your time! Your answers will help us!

User Stories

  • As a client, I want to be able to make an appointment through an app or website so that I won’t be needed to make a phone call.
  • As a client, I want to be able to see salons details so that I can decide faster what suits me best.
  • As a client, I want to be able to rate and review salons so that other users can see these reviews in the future.
  • As a client, I want to be able to select my favorite salons so that I can see them in a convenient place in the future.
  • As a client, I want to be able to see all of my previous appointments and reviews.
  • As a client, I want to be able to view and filter appointments.
  • As an employee, I want to be able to see all my appointments in a calendar.
  • As an employee, I want to be able to add an appointment so that clients can make an appointment right after the current appointment ends.
  • As an employee, I want to be able to filter appointments so that I know how to prepare for what’s next.
  • As an employee, I want to be able to see the reviews for a specific client so that I can decide if I take the appointment or not.
  • As a manager, I want to be able to see all appointments in a calendar.
  • As a manager, I want to be able to filter appointments.
  • As a manager, I want to be able to add an appointment so that clients won’t be required to have an account in order for them to make an appointment.
  • As a manager, I want to be able to edit salon details.
  • As a manager, I want to have an employee management page so that I can add or remove employees or edit the information of current employees.
  • As a manager, I want to have a service management page so that I can add or remove services or edit the details of current services.

Wireframes Updates

According to the interviews with potential users, we decided to update the wireframes as follows.
For authenticated clients, we added two buttons in the salon and employee profile pages: one for contacting the salon / employee to ask for additional details and the other for sharing the salon/ employee to a friend as a recommendation.

Additionally, the clients can see the accumulated vouchers together with their details in a separate view from their profile. The corresponding feature for the salons application is a list of loyal customers. From this view, the salon manager can send custom vouchers to the clients from this list based on the number of recommendations.

Furthermore, for both clients and beauty and wellness centers, we added the option to cancel an appointment. After this update, the salon managers can see their employees' presentation profiles and send edit requests of these.
